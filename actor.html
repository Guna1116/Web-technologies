Dulquer Salmaan

Dulquer Salmaan is an Indian actor, producer, and playback singer who primarily works in the Malayalam film industry. Born on July 28, 1986, in Kochi, Kerala, India, he is the son of renowned actor Mammootty. Dulquer made his acting debut in the 2012 film "Second Show" and gained widespread recognition for his performance. Since then, he has established himself as one of the leading actors in the Malayalam film industry.
